Where to Find Assets

Before you download and use assets

Online stores and crowdsourced libraries are great. But be careful with any online resource like 3D models, audio, or animation. Anything created by a human being is intellectual property and is protected by a legal framework. Copyright laws provide clear directions about use cases for intellectual property.

Read the license of the assets you download. You should be fine using any resource to practice, but do not publish a project that includes assets you did not create, unless the permission to do so is explicit in the license.

Do not let this warning discourage you. There are free-to-use, Creative Commons, CC0, and public domain assets online. If you want to use anything, confirm your permissions and keep a folder with all the licenses you need for any project.

Asset Stores

  • Unity Asset Store↗ is Unity’s official store, where you can find free or paid assets and packages.

  • DevAssets↗ is crowdsourced by developers, their website says: “Pay what you want or grab it for free”.

3D Models

  • Sketchfab↗ has a thriving community of creators and many categories for you to choose new assets that will help you to bring your ideas into existence.

  • Turbo Squid↗ is part of Shutterstock, it provides world-class 3D models.

  • 3DWarehouse↗ works seamlessly with SketchUp. It is specialized in architecture, design, and construction models.

  • Free3D↗ contains a wide range of selections: architecture, animals, anatomy, furniture, food, and much more.

  • Poly Haven↗ is a public library with CC0 (basically public domain, free for any use) models, HDRI, and textures.

Characters Creation

  • ReadyPlayer↗ is a web-based tool to create 3D humanoid characters using a photo.

  • MakeHuman↗ is open-source software to create 3D characters.

Rigged Models and Animation

  • Mixamo↗ is owned by Adobe, and it comes with a long history and a vibrant creator community. In there you will find characters, rigged models, and animation. 

Textures

  • GameTextures↗ contains 3D textures, some of which are customizable. A great texture can breathe life into a plain scene.

  • Poly Haven↗ has, on top of 3D models, a great selection of CC0 textures.

Audio

  • FreesSound↗ contains a vast library of free sound effects.
